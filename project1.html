LOGO DESIGN

In this project, my work is design a logo for a game development company which only design role playing game. This company wants people to show their real self while playing their game. They want to satisfy people’s dream by creating a platform for them to role play different character in their game, and to do something that they can’t in real life, to show their brave side in our game.

PROCESS

STEP1: Research

In order to better understand to design the appropriate logo for the company, I first did the researches about company and its target audience.

STEP2: Brainstorme

Based on the data from the research, I started brainstorming and write down all the design elements and keywords associated with it, and pick up the most relevant words of the company's intentio.Finally did a quick sketch to take down the ideas.

Display

STEP3: Sketches & Draw on illustrator

In this step, based on the previously selected design elements, I began to conceive and sketch as many sketch version as possible and pick up the most competitive logos and to draw with illustrator.

Display

STEP4: Refined version

Choose the best one from the previously works on illustrator according to the display result on screen. Did more version with different colors.

Display

STEP5: Application

Use Photoshop to apply logo on physical objects, and show the physical visual result to the company.

Display

PROBLEMS & CHALLENGES

Company background analysis

It is a big challenge to analyze the company's background which is a necessary step before a designer start design. To understand and analyze the company's background in detail can help me design the appropriate logo for the company. Through the research I learned that the company wanted to stand out from the competition of game companies. For expressing this idea, I tried to use some meaningful stories or myths. I thought of the legend of King Arthur and used "sword in the stone" as the main design element of the logo. It means the beginning of a new world, victory and glory.

Illustrator design

Because of the complexity of draft logos, it is also a challenge to draw them quickly in illustrator. Of course, it is not difficult for me after a lot of exercises. I can not only skillfully use illustrator, but also Photoshop and other design software.

REFLECTION

Designers not only need a good aesthetic and be familiar with software operation, but also need to be able to make accurate judgments on the design direction and customer goal. In the early stage of the design process, research and analysis are essential, and we can not start a design work rashly. In addition, from this work, I also found that a well accumulation of humanistic knowledge is very helpful for the design work. I can associate more design ideas from my previous knowledge accumulation. For example, the legend of King Arthur gave me a lot of design ideas for designing this logo. Therefore, as a designer, I not only need to exercise my design skills, but also need to read more and learn more knowledge in other fields to help me think more comprehensively.
