MAGAZINE DESIGN

In this project, my work is to design a magazine page for a modern fashion article about harajuku style in Japan. This modern fashion article provides some interesting information of harajuku style fashion which is favorite among many young people and lead audience to understand the new trend in modernal fashion today.

PROCESS

STEP1: Research

As the common process of graphic design, I first did the researches about the magazine. The research focus on the field it applaied to and its target audience to help me conceive the appropriate design elements. The research also include the examples about the layout of relevant magazines in order to apply the magazine design style to the current popular trends.

Display

STEP2: brainstorm

Based on my understanding of the theme of magazine and related researches, I started to do brainstorm and draw as many draft layouts as possible. This is a challenging step，I need to do a well layout for a large amount of text information. the layout design need to consider the text readability for the readers and how to arrange the position of informations so that readers can pay attention to important information quickly.

Display

STEP3: Element design

In this design step, I create elements with illustrator accroding to the previous design sketch and consider of the color plate applied to the magazine. I focus on the colors and element applicability. Because this is a fashion magazine about Japanese harajuku style culture, I try to use some related elements, such as Memphis style geometric figures and symbols with brighter colors to cater to the visual preferences of fashion lovers.

Display

STEP4: Layout arrangement with InDesign

In this process, I use InDesign to arrange the overall context on the magazine. InDesign allow me to work with grid and margin, which helps me arrange the magazine design more efficiently. At the same time,In practice, there is often an unpredictable situation. Sometimes the text exceeds the expected layout area. So I did a lot of refine in this step to put all the content into the magazine page appropriately.

Display

STEP5: Final version

I invited some designer peers to view the work and give their feedback. I ask some questions about their reading experience —— how they are feeling? Do these visual designs and layout guide them to read article content effectively? According to the feedback I refined it and finish the final version of magazine finally.

Display

PROBLEMS & CHALLENGES

Layout exploration

The biggest challenge of this design is to do a well layout for a large amount of text information. It is not only need to make the text readable for the readers, but also need to include all the required information within the limited magazine page size. In addition, the example of Banner which I learned from lecture made me realize that I also need to consider the arrangement of the information position to satisfy the user's reading experience so that the readers can find the important information quickly.

Exploration and self creation solutions

Before I drew the framework draft, I self created a collage method which help me to explored the layout before design. First, I found some fashion magazines, and cut some square blocks of similar size as the text area of them. Then I prepared a piece of white paper with the specified size as the background, and put the square blocks on it to explore the layout. This method is very interesting and helps me find some suitable layouts.

REFLECTION

In the early stage of design, I learned a lot from the exploration process of design scheme. In a design process, there are many uncertain factors which I often can not discover until the middle stage of the design. Although I'm happy that I can come up with new ideas, but they have reduced the productivity: I need to spend a lot of time testing new methods for my design. Therefore, targeted research and pre testing can help me effectively narrow my design direction and improve my work efficiency.
