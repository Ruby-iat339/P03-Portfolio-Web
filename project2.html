MAGAZINE DESIGN

In this project, my work is to design a magazine page for a modern fashion article about harajuku style in Japan. This modern fashion article provides some interesting information of harajuku style fashion which is favorite among many young people and lead audience to understand the new trend in modernal fashion today.

PROCESS

STEP1: Research

As the common process of graphic design, I first did the researches about the magazine. The research focus on the field it applaied to and its target audience to help me conceive the appropriate design elements.

Display

STEP2: Layout research

In order to apply the magazine design style to the current popular trends, I did research about the layout of relevant magazines.

STEP3: brainstorm

PROBLEMS & CHALLENGES

Layout exploration

The biggest challenge of this design is to do a well layout for a large amount of text information. It is not only need to make the text readable for the readers, but also need to include all the required information within the limited magazine page size.

Therefore, before I drew the framework draft, I self created a collage method which help me to explored the layout before design. First, I found some fashion magazines, and cut some square blocks of similar size as the text area of them. Then I prepared a piece of white paper with the specified size as the background, and put the square blocks on it to explore the layout. This method is very interesting and helps me find some suitable layouts.

REFLECTION

In the early stage of design, I learned a lot from the exploration process of design scheme. In a design process, there are many uncertain factors which I often can not discover until the middle stage of the design. Although I'm happy that I can come up with new ideas, but they have reduced the productivity: I need to spend a lot of time testing new methods for my design. Therefore, targeted research and pre testing can help me effectively narrow my design direction and improve my work efficiency.
